Our Expertise

Cadastral Surveys

Our surveying services go above and beyond the usual subdivisions, resurveys, amalgamations, change of user, extension of leases, and boundary confirmations, we also provide technical support and conveyancing services for our clients as an added bonus.

Aerial Surveys

At SurveyMI, we leverage advanced drone technology to deliver high-resolution aerial data for precise mapping and analysis. Our licensed drone pilots capture detailed imagery and 3D data efficiently, enabling fast, cost-effective data acquisition for land development, agriculture, environmental monitoring, mining, and infrastructure inspection. Using RTK-enabled drones, we achieve high positional accuracy of up to 3 cm, ensuring reliable data for engineering-grade applications.

Sectional Surveys

At Survey MI we are cognizant of the fact that taking on large scale development projects requires you to be in control of your costs, work flows and expected deliverables. With the real estate market currently on the rise we have tailor made solutions for your needs be it as a developer, advocate or potential unit owner, we have you covered.

Construction Surveys

We offer detailed engineering solutions that assist in the design, planning, execution and monitoring of infrastructure projects. Our services cover topographic surveys, as-built surveys, and construction setting out. We help engineers, architects and contractors optimize construction accuracy. Our team ensures timely delivery of data to move projects from conception to completion.

Geographic Information Systems

We provide comprehensive GIS services including data collection, spatial analysis, and custom mapping solutions. Our GIS experts utilize advanced software to create interactive maps and databases that support decision-making across various sectors such as urban planning, environmental management, and resource allocation.
